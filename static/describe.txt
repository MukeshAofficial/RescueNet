**First Aid Recommendations**

* **Clean the wound:** Gently clean the wound with soap and water.
* **Control bleeding:** Apply pressure to the wound with a clean cloth. If the bleeding is severe, seek medical attention immediately.
* **Cover the wound:** Cover the wound with a clean bandage. 
* **Seek medical attention:** If the wound is deep or if you are concerned about the injury, seek medical attention. 

**Safety Tips for Individuals Affected by a Flood**

* **Stay away from floodwaters:** Floodwaters can be contaminated with sewage, bacteria, and other harmful substances. Avoid contact with floodwaters as much as possible.
* **Be aware of your surroundings:** Be aware of your surroundings and be careful of debris. 
* **Listen to local authorities:** Pay attention to any warnings or advisories issued by local authorities. 
* **Evacuate if necessary:** If you are advised to evacuate, do so immediately. 

**Evacuation Advice**

* **Follow evacuation orders:** If you are ordered to evacuate, do so immediately.
* **Have a plan:** Develop an evacuation plan in advance and practice it with your family. 
* **Gather essential supplies:** Pack a bag with essential supplies, including food, water, clothing, medication, and important documents.
* **Secure your home:** Turn off water, gas, and electricity before evacuating. 
* **Check on your neighbors:** Check on your elderly or disabled neighbors and ensure they have evacuated safely.
